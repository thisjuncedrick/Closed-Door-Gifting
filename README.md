**Stop waiting outside Sebastian's room like a total creep.**

In vanilla Stardew Valley, locked bedroom doors stay locked until 2 hearts. This mod solves that by letting you slip gifts right under locked NPC doors without needing to be invited in, saving you hours of in-game waiting!

### How it Works
Interact with any locked NPC bedroom door while holding a giftable item.

- **If the door is unlocked:** The mod does nothing. The vanilla game takes over as normal.
- **If the NPC is at home & the bedroom door is locked:**
  - The vanilla "locked door" message appears first, followed by a custom prompt asking if you'd like to leave your item as a gift.
  - **Shared Rooms:** "Knocking" on bedroom doors with 2 NPCs (e.g., Robin & Demetrius's bedroom) will prompt you which NPC you want to gift to.
  - The mod verifies your daily and weekly gift limits.
  - Confirm → the item is consumed, friendship increases, based on the set config.

### Features
**1. 100% Vanilla Friendly**  
Door gifts count toward the standard 1-per-day and 2-per-week limits.

**2. Immersive & Silent Deliveries**  
This mod suppresses the vanilla reaction emotes during door-gifting, because nothing breaks immersion quite like a heart emote on top of the NPC you just gifted behind the door.

### Configuration
This mod is fully compatible with the **[Generic Mod Config Menu](https://www.nexusmods.com/stardewvalley/mods/5098)**. You can easily tweak the rules from the main menu or in-game:

- **Enable Mod** (*true*): Turn the mod on or off.
- **Require NPC Met** (*true*): Only allow gifting through doors after you've formally introduced yourself to the NPC.
- **Friendship Multiplier** (*0.25*): Leaving something at a door is arguably less personal than handing it directly. Adjust how much friendship a door gift is worth relative to a normal face-to-face gift. Range: 0.1–1.0.
  *Note: Birthday gifts are strictly governed by vanilla rules!*

### Known Limitations
- **No Business Transactions:** You **cannot** complete "Help Wanted" delivery quests by slipping the requested item under the door. If Haley wants a Super Cucumber, she has to look you in the eye and hand you the gold herself.
- **Expansion Mods:** This mod has been playtested to about 90% coverage in vanilla Stardew Valley. It has *not* been officially tested with massive expansion mods like *[Stardew Valley Expanded](https://www.nexusmods.com/stardewvalley/mods/3753)* or *[Ridgeside Village](https://www.nexusmods.com/stardewvalley/mods/7286)*. In theory, as long as those custom NPCs have standard bedroom doors built like the base game, it should work perfectly!


### Installation
1. Install the latest version of **[SMAPI](https://smapi.io/)**.
2. (Optional but highly recommended) Install **Generic Mod Config Menu** to easily change your settings in game.
3. Download this mod and unzip it into your `Stardew Valley/Mods` folder.
4. Launch the game using SMAPI.

### Uninstallation
1. Delete the mod's folder from your `Stardew Valley/Mods` directory. It is perfectly safe to add or remove mid-playthrough! (Realistically, you'll only need this mod in Year 1, where most of the NPCs are with below 2 hearts.)
